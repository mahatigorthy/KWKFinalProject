Our Mission!

Many public spaces do not provide a safe or empowering environment for women.

It is a universal experience for women to feel unsafe in their daily lives and this negatively affects the wellbeing of women.

More resources and support systems and communities need to be set up and made accessible for women to feel more safe and empowered in their everyday lives. Through Empowher, we hope to create a safe, supportive, and empowering space that all women can come to for resources regarding their safety and mental health.





For women to feel more safe, we've researched and included self defense resources and communities women can use or implement into their lives! The resources include different helpful communities and websites!



We've also provided a collection of mental health hotlines, counselors, and online communities that provide generous support for women feeling unsafe in public environments. With this, we've also added some general self-care ideas to take care of your mental health!



Take action and make a difference in our world! We've combined several resources, including email templates, for you to use your voice.
